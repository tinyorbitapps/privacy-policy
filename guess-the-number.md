# Privacy Policy

**Guess The Number**
**Developed by Tiny Orbit Apps**
**Last Updated: March 20, 2026**

## Introduction

Tiny Orbit Apps ("we", "our", or "us") built the Guess The Number app ("the App") as a free, ad-supported app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

This App is designed to be suitable for all ages, including children under the age of 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google's Families Policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Display Name (Optional):** You may choose a username within the App. If you do not provide one, a random name is generated automatically. This name is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Game Statistics:** We store your gameplay statistics (games played, games won, win streaks, best attempts) locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data does not leave your device unless synced via Google Play Games Services (see below).

- **Firebase Analytics:** The App uses Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google, which may automatically collect certain usage data such as session duration, app opens, device type, operating system version, and general (country/region-level) location derived from your IP address. This information is collected in aggregate and is not used to personally identify you. No custom analytics events are actively tracked.

- **Advertising Data:** The App displays banner advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may automatically collect device identifiers and general device information for the purpose of serving ads. Our ad configuration is set to child-directed treatment, meaning personalized advertising is disabled and only contextual, age-appropriate ads are shown.

### Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

1. **Google AdMob** — Displays banner advertisements.
   - Configured with child-directed treatment (COPPA compliant).
   - No personalized or interest-based ads are served.
   - [Google AdMob Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

2. **Google Play Games Services** — Provides achievements functionality.
   - If you are signed into Google Play Games on your device, your achievement progress is synced to your Google account.
   - We do not access your Google account information beyond what is required for achievements.
   - [Google Play Games Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

3. **Firebase Analytics** — Provides aggregate usage analytics.
   - Collects anonymous usage data (session info, device type, country-level location).
   - No personally identifiable information is collected.
   - [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any of the following:

- Personal information (real name, email address, phone number, physical address)
- Precise location or GPS data
- Photos, videos, or audio recordings
- Contacts, calendar, or files from your device
- Financial or payment information
- Chat messages or communications

## Data Storage and Security

- All user preferences and game data are stored **locally on your device** using SharedPreferences with platform-level encryption.
- No personal data is transmitted to or stored on our servers.
- All communication with third-party services (Firebase, AdMob, Google Play Games) is encrypted via HTTPS.

## Children's Privacy

This App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google's Families Policy:

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
- Advertisements are configured for child-directed treatment — only contextual, age-appropriate ads are displayed.
- No behavioral or interest-based advertising is used.
- The App does not contain any social features, user-to-user communication, or content sharing.
- The App does not request any dangerous permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts, etc.).

## Notifications

The App may send local notifications (daily play reminders and streak reminders) if you grant notification permission. These notifications are generated and scheduled entirely on your device — no push notification services or external servers are involved. You can disable notifications at any time through the App's Settings or your device's system settings.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by:

1. Using the **Reset Progress** option within the App's Settings screen.
2. Uninstalling the App, which removes all local data.
3. For Google Play Games achievement data, visit your [Google Play Games profile settings](https://play.google.com/games/profile).

## Your Choices

- **Audio:** You can enable or disable all app audio from the Settings screen.
- **Notifications:** You can enable or disable notifications from the Settings screen or your device settings.
- **Ad Preferences:** You can manage ad personalization through your device's Google Settings (Settings → Google → Ads).
- **Google Play Games:** Sign-in is automatic if you have Google Play Games on your device. You can manage this through your device's Google Play Games settings.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
